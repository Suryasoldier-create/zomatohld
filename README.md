ZomatoHLD - Food Ordering Platform
ZomatoHLD is a full-stack food ordering and restaurant management application. It allows users to browse local restaurants in Haldwani, place orders, and track their status, while providing restaurant owners with a dashboard to manage their food items and incoming order requests.
